SuperheroMe with IBM Watson


The Idea

Click here for the explanation video

Answering boring quiz questions for hours to find out the superhero inside you is not for Superheroes... Luckily, IBM’s supercomputer, Watson have a solution for them as well, which is:

  • Fast – don’t have to spend any time on answering multiple choice questions
  • Transparent – cheating on the answers is not an option as it is not based on self-reporting
  • Extensive – the result can be anything, as it is not limited to a certain number of superheroes
  • Scientific – the superheroes’ personality is determined with a scientific approach and not by the guesses and impressions of the survey or quiz creator



Check my Profile

In just a few seconds you can get to know who is the superhero inside you. Just upload some self-written English text with at least 100 words – anything, an e-mail or messenger chat you wrote, an article by you, your CV, etc. – and click the SuperheroMe button below.




You can read the result once the personality analysis is done.


Behind the magic

  1. Determining your personality: Several researchers found that variations in word usage in writings such as blogs, essays, and tweets can predict aspects of personality. By analyzing these digital forms of communications, IBM Watson’s Personality Insights service is able to infer individuals' intrinsic personality characteristics. See more info here.
  2. Analyzing Superheroes personality Luckily Superheroes have digital forms of communication as well, which can be found in the form of scripts from movies on IMDB. By downloading these scripts and sending them to IBM Watson’s Personality Insights service it became possible to determine their personality as well.
    • We have Watson analysed the scripts of action movies a between 1977-2013
    • We chose those characters from each movie who had more than 100 words
      • Analyzed the personality of all relevant characters with the IBM Watson’s Personality Insights service
    • The result is fed into a database
  3. Finding the best match: We are comparing your the Big5 traits with the Superheroes' and rank them by whose personality profile is the closest to yours.
